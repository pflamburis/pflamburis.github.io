ABOUT PAUL

PAUL FLAMBURIS will graduate from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst, in 2017 with a Bachelor of Arts in English, a certificate of specialization in Professional Writing and Technical Communication, and a minor in biology. He is interested in scientific writing within the fields of astrobiology, evolutionary biology, genetics, and paleontology. Currently, he is employed as a campus tour guide at Robsham Memorial Center for Visitors. During the summer he performs duties as a program assistant at the Juniper Institute for Young Writers.

During his off time he enjoys writing and reading science fiction, going to the movies, jogging, and daydreaming about the colonization of Mars. His least favorite dinosaur is triceratops, and his least favorite holidays are Easter, April Fools' Day, and National Peanut Butter & Jelly Day.
